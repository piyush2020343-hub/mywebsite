About Me

Hello! I am Piyush Acharya. Welcome to my personal website. Piyush Acharya is a researcher of Modern Indian History, currently completing his Master of Arts in History at the University of Delhi, where he also earned his Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in History. His academic trajectory is anchored in the critical examination of colonial socio-religious dynamics, subaltern histories, and the complex ideological frameworks of twentieth-century nationalist discourse. Research Specialization & Historiographical Focus Acharya s research methodology emphasizes close archival analysis and the interrogation of structural power dynamics in colonial India. His primary areas of scholarly interest include: Colonial Social Reform & Archival Histories: Analyzing the legal, social, and rhetorical dimensions of colonial interventions, with a specific focus on the documentation, resistance, and discourse surrounding institutionalized practices like Sati. Marginalized & Pastoralist Historiographies: Reconstructing the socio-economic and cultural histories of indigenous and pastoral communities, such as the Bhil tribe and the migratory camel-rearing communities (Rabari and Fakirani Jat) of Western India, challenging mainstream state-centric narratives. Nationalist Ideologies: Investigating the political thought, strategic debates, and interpersonal socio-political dynamics between key figures of the Indian national movement. Academic Background & Credentials Master of Arts in History | University of Delhi (Final Year) Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in History | University of Delhi (Graduated 2024) Foundational Education: Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) Hurda Technical & Extracurricular Competencies: RS-CIT Certified (Information Technology proficiency) and National Cadet Corps (NCC) 'A' Certificate holder. Career Objective Aspirant for advanced doctoral research, Piyush is currently leveraging his comprehensive training in historiography and source criticism to transition into roles as a Research Assistant. इतिहास) | दिल्ली विश्वविद्यालय (उत्तीर्ण 2024) प्रारंभिक शिक्षा: जवाहर नवोदय विद्यालय (JNV) हुर्दा तकनीकी एवं पाठ्येतर योग्यताएं: RS-CIT प्रमाणित (सूचना प्रौद्योगिकी दक्षता) और राष्ट्रीय कैडेट कोर (NCC) 'A' प्रमाणपत्र धारक। करियर का उद्देश्य उच्च स्तरीय डॉक्टरेट (Ph.D.) अनुसंधान के आकांक्षी, पियूष वर्तमान में इतिहासलेखन और स्रोत समीक्षा (source criticism) के अपने व्यापक प्रशिक्षण का उपयोग अनुसंधान सहायक (Research Assistant) के रूप में कार्य करने के लिए कर रहे हैं। उनका दीर्घकालिक व्यावसायिक लक्ष्य शिक्षा जगत को समर्पित है, जिसके तहत वे इतिहास के सहायक प्राध्यापक (Assistant Professor) के रूप में उच्च शिक्षा में योगदान देना चाहते हैं, ताकि उपाश्रित और आधुनिक दक्षिण एशियाई ऐतिहासिक अनुसंधान के दायरों का विस्तार किया जा सके।
